Instructions

BOPs 0x0X

  • NOP: 0x0000
  • INC: 0x010R
  • DEC: 0x020R
  • AND: 0x03RR
  • OR: 0x04RR
  • XOR: 0x05RR
  • RSH: 0x06RR
  • LSH: 0x07RR
